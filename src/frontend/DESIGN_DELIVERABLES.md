# TaskQuest Design Deliverables

## 1. App Structure & Navigation Flow

### Screen Hierarchy
- **Landing Page** (unauthenticated)
  - Hero section with mascot
  - Feature highlights
  - CTA to login

- **Authenticated App Shell**
  - Header with logo, user profile, login/logout
  - Sidebar navigation (desktop) / hamburger menu (mobile)
  - Main content area
  - Footer with attribution

### Core Screens
1. **Home Dashboard** - Daily overview, next task, quick stats, quick actions
2. **Tasks** - Task list with CRUD, filtering, completion flows
3. **Reminder Center** - Active and upcoming reminders
4. **Rewards** - XP/level progress, coins, badges, weekly chest
5. **Story Mode** - Chapter progression
6. **Avatar** - Avatar customization and progression stages
7. **Quests** - Daily quests and boss challenges
8. **Leaderboard** - Personal stats and progress tracking
9. **Insights** - Productivity patterns and suggestions
10. **History** - Completion records with proofs and reflections
11. **Settings** - Theme, audio, motivation style, onboarding reset
12. **Onboarding** - Multi-step first-run setup
